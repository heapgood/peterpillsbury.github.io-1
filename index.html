Welcome & Introduce

Hello! my name is Peter!

Principal, Product Manager at Core Logic & Part-time UX/UI+Code Bootcamp Instructor at UC Irvine. Prior to Core Logic was the Senior UX/UI Product Director / Creative Director for Real Estate Digital in charge of media and data solutions to real estate franchisors, brokerages, agents and MLS organizations and associations. Berkshire Hathaway Home Services, Long & Foster, ReeceNichols, Homeseekers, Bank of America & MRIS, Xome, Mr Cooper just to name a few. Before that, Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, as the Head of User Experience, leading a team of health care IT professionals through the daily tasks of developing multiple web based applications servicing over 14,000 employees as well as public users.
